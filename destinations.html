Maasai Mara National Reserve

Home to the Great Migration - one of the Seven Natural Wonders of Africa

The Maasai Mara is Kenya's most famous wildlife reserve, known for its exceptional population of lions, leopards, cheetahs, and the annual wildebeest migration. The reserve is named after the Maasai people and the Mara River that flows through it.

Highlights:

  • Witness the Great Migration (July to October)
  • Spot the Big Five: lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino
  • Experience authentic Maasai culture
  • Take a hot air balloon safari at dawn

Best Time to Visit:

June to October (dry season and migration), though the Mara is excellent for wildlife viewing year-round.

Tsavo National Park

Kenya's largest national park, divided into Tsavo East and Tsavo West

Tsavo is renowned for its vast wilderness and biodiversity. The park is famous for its red elephants, which appear red because they dust themselves with the park's distinctive red soil. Tsavo East offers great wildlife viewing in a semi-arid environment, while Tsavo West features more varied landscapes including volcanic hills and natural springs.

Highlights:

  • See the famous red elephants
  • Visit Mzima Springs, a natural oasis with underwater viewing chamber
  • Explore the Shetani Lava Flows
  • Enjoy spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro from certain points

Best Time to Visit:

June to October and January to February (dry seasons) for the best wildlife viewing.

Lake Nakuru National Park

Famous for its flamingos and diverse bird species

Lake Nakuru National Park is centered around the shallow alkaline lake that attracts thousands of flamingos, creating a stunning pink border around the lake's edge. Beyond the flamingos, the park is home to both black and white rhinos, making it one of the best places in Kenya to see these endangered species.

Highlights:

  • Witness the spectacular flamingo flocks (seasonal)
  • Spot both black and white rhinos
  • Visit Baboon Cliff for panoramic views
  • See Rothschild's giraffes and other wildlife

Best Time to Visit:

June to February is generally good, with the dry seasons (June to October and January to February) offering the best wildlife viewing.
