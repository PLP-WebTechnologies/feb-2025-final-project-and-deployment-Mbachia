Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Kenya for safari?

The best time for wildlife viewing is during the dry seasons (June to October and January to February) when animals gather around water sources. The Great Migration in Maasai Mara happens from July to October.

How many days do I need for a good safari experience?

We recommend at least 5-7 days to experience Kenya's diverse wildlife and landscapes. This allows you to visit 2-3 different parks or reserves without feeling rushed.

What should I pack for a safari?

Essential items include neutral-colored clothing (avoid bright colors), a hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, insect repellent, comfortable walking shoes, a light jacket for early mornings, and a camera with extra batteries and memory cards.

Do I need vaccinations to visit Kenya?

Yellow fever vaccination is required for entry into Kenya. Other recommended vaccinations include hepatitis A and B, typhoid, and malaria prophylaxis. Please consult with your healthcare provider before traveling.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Yes, we can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. Please inform us of your needs when booking so we can make appropriate arrangements.
